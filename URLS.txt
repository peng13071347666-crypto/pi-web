pi-web daily URL
================

http://localhost:30142

One-click startup
=================

Double-click:
C:\Users\彭宏宣\pi-web\启动脚本\一键启动 pi-web 30142.bat

Use it when http://localhost:30142 cannot open.

Notes
=====

- The script starts pi-web as a background service process.
- Closing the startup window will not stop the website.
- Runtime logs are stored in:
  C:\Users\彭宏宣\pi-web\启动脚本\logs
- Old startup scripts were moved to:
  C:\Users\彭宏宣\pi-web\启动脚本\old-startup-scripts-20260704
